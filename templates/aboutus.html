About us

NEUROMER is a dynamic organization founded by medical students and comprised of neurology and neurosurgery experts. Our mission is to bring together medical students from all over the world, providing them with opportunities to expand their knowledge and experience in the fields of neurology and neurosurgery. Through collaboration with professionals, we aim to drive positive change and advancements in neurological care.
